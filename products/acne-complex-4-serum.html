Simple Routine

How to Use

1

Cleanse

Wash your face with a gentle cleanser and pat dry. We recommend the Clear Glow Exfoliating Soap.

2

Apply

Dispense 2–3 drops onto fingertips and apply to affected areas or full face.

3

Massage

Gently press and pat the serum into skin until fully absorbed. Do not rub harshly.

4

Protect

In the morning, always follow with SPF. At night, follow with your moisturiser.
