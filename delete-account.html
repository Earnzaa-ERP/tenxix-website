Delete Your Kira Account

Remove your account and all personal data permanently — two ways to do it.

If you'd like to delete your Kira account and all the personal data associated with it, you have two options.

Option 1 — In-App (fastest)

If you still have the Kira app installed:

  1. Open Kira on your phone
  2. Go to Settings → Delete Account
  3. Confirm when prompted
Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2 — Email Us

If you no longer have the app installed, or you'd prefer not to use the in-app option, send us an email:

  • To: support@tenxix.com
  • Subject: Delete my Kira account
  • Include:
    • The email address you used to sign up
    • Your full name (so we can verify identity)

We will confirm receipt within 3 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days of the verified request.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your name and email address
  • All skin scan photos you have uploaded
  • All skin analysis results and progress history
  • Your skincare quiz answers and routine tracking data
  • Your push notification token

What May Be Retained

In limited cases, we may retain some information:

  • Anonymised, aggregated statistics that cannot identify you (used for improving the service)
  • Records required by law (such as financial records if you ever made a purchase), retained only for the period required by Nigerian law

Need Help?

If you don't receive confirmation within 3 business days, or if you have any questions about the deletion process, contact us at support@tenxix.com.
